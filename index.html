Research interests

My work is organized around a practical question: how can intelligent software remain inspectable, verifiable, and accountable?

  • Trustworthy AI for codeCode provenance, watermarking, automated repair, and evaluation of AI-assisted development.
  • Program analysis and securityStatic, symbolic, and hybrid analysis for C, WebAssembly, and smart contracts.
  • Responsible software intelligenceCompliance benchmarks, software agents, and reproducible empirical methods.
