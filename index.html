2026AI provenance01

The Watermark Paradox: How Provenance Verification Paves the Road to Camouflaged Backdoors

Haoyi Zhang, Huaijin Ran, Kisub Kim, Xunzhu Tang

ACM FSE 2026 · Ideas, Visions & Reflections

A critical reflection on code watermarking: provenance markers and backdoors share the same structural pattern—hidden signals embedded while semantics remain unchanged. The paper argues for shifting trust from statistical markers toward functional verification.

2026Accountable repair02

Who Wrote This Patch? Toward Accountable Automated Program Repair

Huaijin Ran, Haoyi Zhang, Kisub Kim, Xunzhu Tang

ACM FSE 2026 · Ideas, Visions & Reflections

A research agenda for accountability in LLM-based program repair. It introduces multi-patch fingerprinting, distributing provenance across a portfolio of candidate repairs through behavioral signatures and opportunistic embedding.

2026Smart contracts03

SPECTRA: Specification-Oriented Static Analysis for Smart Contract Security and Behavioral Contract Inference

Haoyi Zhang, Tianfeng Liang, Huaijin Ran, Md Maruf Hasan, Xunzhu Tang

ACM FSE Companion 2026 · Two-page companion paper

A Solidity analysis toolkit combining symbolic execution, abstract interpretation, SMT verification, and CEGAR to infer Hoare-style behavioral specifications and surface access-control, reentrancy, overflow, and unchecked-call risks.

Haoyi Zhang, Tianfeng Liang, Huaijin Ran, Md Maruf Hasan, Xunzhu Tang

Haoyi Zhang, Huaijin Ran, Tianfeng Liang, Md Maruf Hasan, Xunzhu Tang

2026Spatio-temporal ML06

MSESTA: Multi-Scale Evolving Spatial-Temporal Attention for Long-Term Traffic Prediction

Huaijin Ran, Haoyi Zhang

Neural Networks · Online / In Press

A linear-complexity evolving attention model that moves through summarization, aggregation, and broadcasting across multiple temporal scales. Evaluations on four PEMS datasets target stable and efficient 2–7 hour traffic forecasting.

2025Compliance benchmark07

GDPR-Bench-Android: A Benchmark for Evaluating Automated GDPR Compliance Detection in Android

Huaijin Ran, Haoyi Zhang, Xunzhu Tang

arXiv preprint · cs.SE

A benchmark of 1,951 manually annotated violations from 15 open-source Android repositories, spanning 23 GDPR articles and three code granularities. It compares 11 LLM, RAG, ReAct, and Formal-AST approaches across localization and multi-label classification.

2025LLM compliance08

Can Large Language Models Detect Real-World Android Software Compliance Violations?

Haoyi Zhang, Huaijin Ran, Xunzhu Tang

arXiv preprint · cs.SE

CompliBench evaluates code-level auditing under LGPD, PDPA, and PIPEDA. Its stability-aware metrics test whether models remain consistent across file, module, line, and jurisdictional views—not merely whether one prediction is correct.

2025Agentic SE09

Intelligent Agents for Software Engineering: A Systematic Literature Review

Haoyi Zhang, Huaijin Ran, Xunzhu Tang

Author preprint

A PRISMA-style review of 215 studies selected from 400 records, covering April 2023–July 2025. It organizes agent architectures and software-engineering tasks while mapping gaps in repository-scale evaluation, safety, cost, latency, and human–AI collaboration.
